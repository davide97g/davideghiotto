My Investing Strategy

Passive

As long as I like making money, I am not willing to spend too much time following the stock market. It's not my passion.
But, in my opinion, investments are a great tool that can be exploited in several ways: the one that attracts me the most is the passive investing strategy : consistently investing over time in good companies and diversified. ETFs.

Growth

My goal now is to grow my capital to 50k within 2 years.
The deadline is Dec 2022.
My current risk attitude is high for several reasons:

  • Young
  • Financially stable
  • Debt free

For these reasons I feel like to invest a small percentage of my portfolio in more risky stocks ( e.g. in genomics ) that may payoff well in the distant future.
I found these risky stocks following ARK Invest ideas for disrupting innovation.

Trust

It's fundamental not to lose money investing in the stock market. For me it's important to have someone who can explain complex things and guide me to more reasonable choices.
This financial journey started thanks to Darko Ivanovski and his YouTube Channel. You can find everything you need and more there!

How I collect
Stocks Data

My broker does not created awesome statistics or graphs, so I just created them myself.
If you want to see the code behind these pages, soon I will make my repository public.



The problem was simple:

"I need to get the data of my current portfolio"


But, as a programmer, I want to automate this task.
We can identify 4 steps in order to recreate this web page:

  1. Scrape data from the broker website: Selenium Python
  2. Save data into a database: MongoDB cloud collections
  3. Retrieve data on demand: Heroku running Flask app
  4. Schedule the scraping: MongoDB triggers

In case you wondered, I use DEGIRO as my broker.
